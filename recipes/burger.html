Burger Recipe

Description

This recipe has very few ingredients; all you need is some ground beef, cheese, and vegetables of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Place the ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
  3. Drain grease, and place the beef in a bowl. Mix in the cheese and vegetables.
  4. Place the mixture into a 9x9 inch baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove the foil, and bake an additional 15 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
